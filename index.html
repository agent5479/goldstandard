The philosophy

Healthy habits for dogs
and their humans.

Dogs find peace and freedom when they know their place and learn trust and obedience. Gold Standard uses the core techniques and philosophy of Beckman's dog training school — patient, structured methods that work with how dogs actually think.

Training the owner is key. Warwick coaches you through each step so you can maintain the results at home with confidence instead of second-guessing.

What you can achieve

  • Consistent recall
  • Slack-leash walking
  • Impulse control
  • No more jumping
  • Calm, trusting behaviour

How it works

The approach

Gold Standard training is built around one principle: a dog that understands its place in the relationship is a relaxed, happy dog. That calm comes from structure — not punishment, not permissiveness, but clear and consistent guidance.

Sessions are held in Golden Bay and are tailored to the dog and the owner. The goal is always to give you the tools to keep the training going long after the session ends.

"Both pet and owner's needs are met — and stress is reduced."
